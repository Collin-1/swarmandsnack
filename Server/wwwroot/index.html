How to Play

  • Objective: Eat the opponent's swarm before they eat yours!
  • Controls: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your Leader.
  • Mechanics: Your Leader (big circle) guides the swarm. Collide with smaller enemy dots to eat them.
  • Winning: If you run out of underlings, you lose. The last player standing wins!
